Epic Fusion is a Canadian technology company with significant experience providing automation solutions to Chinese aerospace and automotive manufacturers through our Epic-HUST joint venture with Huazhong Univeristy of Science and Technology.

Investments & Partnerships

The company is now leveraging this experience in China by evolving into a technology investment company. Epic Fusion will invest and partner with private Canadian technology companies and market their products in China through our strategic Chinese academic and industry partners.

China Market Expansion

In cooperation with the China Canada Emerging Technologies Park under development in in Langfang, Hebei Province, Epic Fusion will provide a unique base for Canadian organizations interested in entering or expanding operations in China. Strategically located between Beijing and Tianjin, and close to the new Beijing airport opening in 2019, the China Canada Emerging Technologies Park will benefit companies involved in these sectors:

1) Clean Technology
2) Aerospace & Earth Observation
3) Life Sciences
